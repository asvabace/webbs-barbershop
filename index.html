Our Story

About the Shop

WEBB$ BARBER SHOP is a full-service, veteran-owned barbershop in the heart of Lebanon, Tennessee, dedicated to consistently providing a high level of customer experience and satisfaction.

Founded by Timothy Webb, a proud veteran of the United States military, WEBB$ BARBER SHOP was built on the same principles of discipline, precision, and attention to detail that defined his service. Located at 520 W Main Street, we offer expert haircuts, detailed fades, beard grooming, and styling services in a relaxing, professional atmosphere.

Whether you're a regular or walking in for the first time, our goal is simple: you leave looking and feeling your absolute best. Every. Single. Time.
